Two Months

03 January 2014

Two Months.

That is all that is left of this adventure called gSchool.

It’s almost unreal to think… it’s already been four months since we were all thrown into a room with Jessie Shternshus from the Improv Effect. Pushed out of our comfort zones and pushed into (rather amazing) group building activities.

Now, four months later, that day seems like a dream of some other time. Years ago, even.

The day in day out pushing and being pushed has grown into the norm. We’ve become accustomed to seeing each other’s smiling (or very very tired) faces. Friendships have blossomed and been cultivated by this experience.

It’s amazing what clear vision, a common goal, and constanty opportunity can do to a group of people. You start as strangers and transition into family.

It will be interesting to see, as these next 8 weeks unravel and provide job opportunities – and new life opportunities – where we all end up.
